About

I am a dedicated 17-year-old high-honors student at Frankfurt International School in Oberursel, Germany, with a passion for computer science and a strong academic track record. As an all-season athlete, I actively participate in volleyball, basketball, and track, showcasing my commitment and team spirit. My involvement in community service through Roots Revive and Youth Against Prejudice reflects my dedication to making a positive impact by raising awareness about deforestation and combating prejudice. I possess strong analytical and critical thinking skills, proficiency in Java and Golang programming languages, and working knowledge of Python and HTML. My excellent organizational and interpersonal skills make me a great team player and an effective leader.
